The Web

And Infrastructure

CIT-82 is a class offered by Fresno City College which discusses and disects the various web technologies in play today on the modern web.
This site is an online collection of information and knowledge I've gained during my time in the class.
Happy Browsing! - Jeffrey M.

HTTPS

HTTPS is the primary protocol used to form a secure connection between a client's browser and a website on the World Wide Web.

FTP

Need to transfer large amounts of data between two computers on a network? FTP is the tried and true method. When getting up off your butt to hand someone a thumbdrive is too much to ask.

Dark Web

The Dark Web is the portion of the internet that cannot be accessed unless with special software. On the Dark Web you can find illegal content and black markets.

Web 2.0

Web 2.0 is the progression of the web from mainly static webpages to interactive dynamic content. The user's of a site are often encouraged to join in the creation and cultivation of content.

UTF-8

There is one Character Encoding on the web to rule them all. At least in the current popularity contest UTF-8 has the lion share of the market. And for Good reason.
